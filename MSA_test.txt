MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("MSA") is entered into by and between Acme Corp and Tech Solutions.

ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS
"Services" means the software development and IT consulting services provided by Tech Solutions.
"Confidential Information" means any proprietary data shared between the parties.

ARTICLE 2: OBLIGATIONS
Tech Solutions must deliver the software source code within 30 days of project commencement.
Acme Corp shall pay the invoice within 15 days of receipt.

ARTICLE 3: LIABILITIES
Tech Solutions' total liability under this MSA shall not exceed $10,000.
Tech Solutions agrees to indemnify Acme Corp against any third-party intellectual property claims.
